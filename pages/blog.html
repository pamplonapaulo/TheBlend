The açaí anti-aging properties

Scientific researchers have been encouraging the cosmetic industry to include açaí in their chemical inventory.

One of the most popular bowls in San Diego, the acaí blend now can also be considered a great source of anthocyanin, a spectacular antioxidant pigment that fights aging. The information is from the Amazon National Research Institute, in Brazil.

The French cosmetics company L'Oréal, for instance, is one of those who use the purple Brazilian berry in it's formulas. "When infused into cosmetic formulas, acai constituents possess the potential to improve skin appearance by supplying antioxidant properties. Antioxidant protection works to protect the skin by neutralizing cell-damaging free radicals present in the environment. This effect may even help to prolong the integrity and quality of skincare products" says the company's web-page.
