We love bowls and smoothies

The Blend Superfood Bar

Photo courtesy of the owner

We are a growing family fanatic about bowls and smoothies.

Our passion started when Matt and Megan traveled to an exciting adventure in the Amazon, South America. There they found açaí, a miraculous Brazilian berry rich in energy nutrients, like protein, vitamins C, B1 and B2. Just 100 grams have up to 250 calories!

Matt and Megan met each other during a trip to Machu Picchu, Peru, exploring the mystical stories of the ancient society of the Incas.

They got married one year later. As a revival of that first experience, they returned to South America where they found their first açaí bowl, and it changed their destiny forever.

Back in San Diego, Matt and Megan were encouraged by their kids to create The Blend Superfood Bar, so in 1998 this family started to grow this business with plenty of affection and good will for our community - which we believe are our most important ingredients.
